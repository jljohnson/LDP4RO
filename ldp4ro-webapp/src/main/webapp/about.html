About LDP4ROs

This page is a prototype for creating Research Objects [1] with the Linked Data Platform [2]. The project is currently under development on Github: https://github.com/oeg-upm/LDP4RO, based on the alignment proposed in http://purl.org/net/ldp4ro/spec

In order to create a Research Object, go here and just drop your files, the requested metadata and your Research Object will be ready to go.

Created by Daniel Garijo and Nandana Mihindukulasooriya.

References

  1. Khalid Belhajjame, Jun Zhao, Daniel Garijo, Matthew Gamble, et al. Suite of Ontologies for Preserving Workflow-Centric Research Objects. To appear in Journal of Web Semantics.
  2. S. Speicher, J. Arwe, and A. Malhotra. Linked data platform 1.0. w3c proposed recommendation 16 december 2014. Technical report, W3C, 2014
